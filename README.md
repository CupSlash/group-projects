# RPG Character Manager

This RPG character manager allows you to manage avatars for a Role-Playing Game, hence the name. Characters have a name, class, race, level, and field of attributes, just like those in Dungeons & Dragons. Characters are also given a random side quest, backstory, and piece equipment upon creation. You can later edit levels, attributes, and skills. A range of analyses is available for each of your characters and you may also compare your characters to one another.

## Menu Options

Create a character

- Creating a character is easy. The program asks you a series of questions about your character and you provide answers. First, you'll be asked about the name of your new character. You can choose to have it generate a random name if you'd like. Then, you'll choose their race and class. Third, you must tell the program the character's level ranging from 1 to 20. Fourth, you must select an attribute template for your character or manually configure attributes yourself. Finally, select your character's first skill.

Edit a character

- _Edit a character_ gives you three options. You can edit your character's inventory, level, or skills. 

Compare characters

- When this option is selected, you will be asked to choose two characters out of a list. Once both characters have been selected, a graph will appear showing side-by-side bars for each stat.

View characters

- This option redirects you to a submenu, allowing you to view all your characters, filter through them by attribute, or choose to sort by a stat.

Attribute display

- _Attribute display_ consists of multiple menu options. The _view statistical analysis_ option shows you the mean, median, max, and min for every attribute across characters. _Character progression display_ presents a graph showing the level of each attribute since the creation of one of your characters. When you tell the program to display a character's stats, it presents a similar graph showing a character's current stats.

Exit

- Exit is pretty straight forward. When you select this option, the application stops.
